Pro se petitioner Lorenzo Arteaga seeks leave to proceedin forma pauperis to file a petition for a writ of certiorari tothe Ninth Circuit. The Ninth Circuit affirmed the DistrictCourt's dismissal with prejudice of petitioner's complaint forfailure to amend his complaints pursuant to the DistrictCourt's instructions.We deny petitioner leave to proceedin forma pauperis.He is allowed until March 16, 1998, within which to pay thedocketing fee required by Rule 38 and to submit his petitionin compliance with Rule 33.1.
